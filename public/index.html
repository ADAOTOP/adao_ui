New Invest to Earn DAO Community Autonomy


A-DAO will be based on dApp staking of Astar Network. Users will get some of the developer rewards while participating and gaining basic rewards. At present, A-DAO divides the developer rewards into: Revenue Reward, On-chain Treasury, Construction Bonus, any rewards of which can be adjusted by DAO governance.

  • Revenue Reward

    60% of developer revenue will be distributed to all users who participate in staking Astar, for A-DAO itself was created to allow everyone to get more income.

  • On-chain Treasury

    30% of the developer revenue will be allocated to the on-chain treasury, which will first be used to invest in ecological projects on Astar, including but not limited to: project investment, liquidity mining, etc.

  • Construction Bonus

    10% of the developer revenue will be allocated to the project. After the establishment of the A-DAO committee, anyone who provides assistance to the construction of A-DAO can apply for this bonus.

A DAO that redefines the investment to earn mechanism. stay tuned!
